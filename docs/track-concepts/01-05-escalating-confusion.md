# Tracks 01–05: Escalating Confusion + Descent Begins

## Album Arc Position
- **01–04**: Escalating confusion. Disoriented → unhinged. Mid-tempo to fast.
- **05**: First track of the "sinking trilogy" (05–07). Heavy, deliberate.

---

## Track 01: "wtf"

**Concept:** The album opens mid-confusion. A riff starts like it knows where it's going, then the harmony lurches sideways. The listener lands in the middle of something already falling apart.

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Key | E minor (Phrygian) |
| Tempo | 138 BPM |
| Time Sig | 4/4 |
| Bass | Electric Bass Pick (34) |
| Melody | Distortion Guitar (30) |
| Chords | Overdriven Guitar (29) |
| Directive | Rhythmic Displacement (melody shifted 1 eighth note) |

**Structure:**
1. 4 bars — driving emo, full band, everything locked
2. 4 bars — melody displaced by 8th note, chords shift to sus2 (unsettled)
3. 4 bars — everything locks back in, louder (+15 velocity)
4. 2 bars — sudden gap, just one floor tom hit
5. 2 bars — crash back at max velocity

**Theory:**
- Progression: i–bII–v–i (E Phrygian, the b2 = F natural = dread)
- Melody: descending contour with min7 spike at bar 9
- Bass: locks with kick first 4 bars, chromatic approach into root at bar 12

## Track 04: "omfg"

**Concept:** The peak of unhinged. Structure barely holds. Two completely different songs crammed into 30 seconds with a noise transition.

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Key | Song A: D minor / Song B: F# minor (tritone apart) |
| Tempo | Song A: 110 BPM / Song B: 148 BPM |
| Time Sig | Song A: 3/4 (woozy waltz) / Song B: 4/4 (violent snap-to) |
| Bass | Electric Bass Finger (33) |
| Melody | Overdriven Guitar (29) |
| Chords | Song A: Pad Warm (89) / Song B: Distortion Guitar (30) |
| Directive | Two Songs Stitched |

**Structure:**
1. ~6 bars Song A — slow waltz, descending melody, wide open voicings (~10s)
2. ~1 bar transition — cluster + arrhythmic drums + random velocity bass (~2s)
3. ~10 bars Song B — fast aggressive, rising melody, power chords (~18s)

**Theory:**
- Song A: i–VI–III–VII in Dm, Dorian color (B natural)
- Song B: i–iv–bII–i in F#m, Phrygian aggression
- Tritone key relationship makes the transition feel like falling through the floor

## Track 05: "shit"

**Concept:** The descent begins. First of the sinking trilogy. Heavy, deliberate, like wading through something thick. Establishes a motif that tracks 06 and 07 will degrade.

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Key | A minor (natural minor) |
| Tempo | 108 BPM |
| Time Sig | 4/4 |
| Bass | Electric Bass Finger (33) |
| Melody | Distortion Guitar (30) |
| Chords | Overdriven Guitar (29) |
| Directive | The Drone (E1 sustained entire track) + The Collapse |

**Structure:**
1. 4 bars — half-time drums, chords only, establishing motif (i–III–VII–iv)
2. 4 bars — melody enters descending, bass locks with floor tom pulse
3. 4 bars — full band driving emo, melody repeats motif higher
4. 2 bars — The Collapse: just bass playing motif alone, fragile
5. 1 bar — everything crashes back in fortissimo

**Theory:**
- Core motif: i–III–VII–iv with add9 voicings (shimmery sad emo)
- Melody contour: A5 → descending minor scale → lands on E4 (the 5th)
- Bass ghost notes throughout for physical weight
- Drone on E1 (MIDI 28) provides subliminal tension
- This motif will be reused and degraded in tracks 06 and 07
